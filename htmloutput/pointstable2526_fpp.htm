Points Table for Members - First Past the Post

Last updated 08 Oct 25 21:40

This table shows the current standing in the seasonal first past the post points competiton after 8 races. Points are scored by members in every race according to the Rinderle B high point scoring system.

Points are scored according to race position and the number of other boats in the race.
Race position is determined by first past the post - no handicap is applied.
You must be a financial member of MPYC to score points.
Non members and members from affiliated clubs cannot score points but can push members down the point tables.
