Points Table for Members

Last updated 03 Aug 16 19:50

This table shows the current standing in the seasonal points competiton. Points are scored by members in every race according to the formula points = 300 / (2 + position). So if you come first in a race you score 100 points, second 75 and so forth. Position is done according to PYC handicap numbers.
You must be a financial member of MPYC to score points. Non members and other members from affiliated clubs cannot score points but do push other members down the rankings.
