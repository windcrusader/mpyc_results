Points Table for Members

Last updated 13 Apr 25 19:11

This table shows the current standing in the seasonal points competiton after 113 races. Points are scored by members in every race according to the Rinderle B high point scoring system.

Points are scored according to race position and the number of other boats in the race.
Race position is determined according to corrected time based on PY 2020 numbers.
You must be a financial member of MPYC to score points.
Non members and members from affiliated clubs cannot score points but can push members down the point tables.

Note that MPYC is trialling a new "nominated" sail size scoring system this season. All results in the season table are recalculated with the handicap from the largest sail the sailor has used in the season so far regardless of what sail was used on a particular day.
