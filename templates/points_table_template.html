This table shows the current standing in the seasonal points competiton. Points are scored by members in every race according to the formula points = 300 / (2 + position).
If you come first in a race you score 100 points, second 75 and so forth. Race position is determined according to corrected time based on PY 2016 numbers.
You must be a financial member of MPYC to score points. Non members and members from affiliated clubs cannot score points but can push members down the individual race rankings.
