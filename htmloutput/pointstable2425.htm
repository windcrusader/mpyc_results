Points Table for Members

Last updated 28 Sep 24 10:32

This table shows the current standing in the seasonal points competiton after 8 races. Points are scored by members in every race according to the Rinderle B high point scoring system.

Points are scored according to race position and the number of other boats in the race.
Race position is determined according to corrected time based on PY 2019 numbers.
You must be a financial member of MPYC to score points.
Non members and members from affiliated clubs cannot score points but can push members down the point tables.
